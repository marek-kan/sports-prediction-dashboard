NBA, NHL Predictions

Full-time, handicap probabilities in the tables are from home team perspective.

Total score line probabilities in the tables are from over the line perspective.

Baseline accuracy means:

  • Always pick home to win
  • Always home to cover the hcp (NBA) or always away to cover hcp (NHL)
  • Always pick over in totals.
